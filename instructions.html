Mad Libs

A mad-lib game created during Cohort 43 at chingu.

How To Play

  1. Guess the a word following the hints on your input entry.
  2. Each guess must not be a curse word.
  3. Once you fill out the word form click submit to view the full story filled with your word guess.
  4. Play the game in social situations, it meant to be funny by constructing silly sentenses.
